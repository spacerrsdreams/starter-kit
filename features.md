Features:

1. Authentication fully supported: sign up, sign in, Google login, sign out.
2. Welcome and account emails are supported: email verification and reset password email flow.
3. Password reset flow works end to end: request reset link and set new password.
4. Dashboard layout is implemented with desktop sidebar and mobile navigation.
5. Command menu is available (Cmd/Ctrl + K) for quick navigation and actions.
6. AI chat page is implemented with chat creation, messaging, and saved conversation history.
7. AI chat history management is supported: open old chats, create new chats, copy chat links, delete chats.
8. Floating AI widget is integrated globally with compact chat access and history dropdown.
9. Settings dialog is implemented with account, notifications, appearance, and legal sections.
10. Dark mode and theme switching are supported across the app.
11. Notification preferences are supported (marketing/personalized email opt-in and updates).
12. Account deactivation flow is implemented with feedback submission and session invalidation.
13. Public legal pages are available: Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
